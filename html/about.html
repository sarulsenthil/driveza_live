About Driveza

Your trusted partner in quality used vehicles in the Bay Area since 2008

Our Story

DriveZA was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide Bay Area families with access to quality, affordable used vehicles. What started as a small family business in Livermore has grown into one of the most trusted names in the used car industry.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of customers find their perfect vehicle, whether it's a reliable family car, a fuel-efficient commuter, or a rugged workhorse. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer service has earned us a reputation for excellence.

Today, we continue to uphold the values that made us successful: honesty, integrity, and a genuine passion for helping people find the right car for their needs and budget.

Our Values

Trust & Transparency

We believe in complete honesty about every vehicle's history, condition, and pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Quality First

Every vehicle undergoes rigorous inspection and testing to ensure it meets our high standards before it reaches our lot.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We're committed to providing exceptional service and support.

Fair Pricing

We offer competitive, market-based pricing with transparent valuations to make quality vehicles accessible to everyone.
