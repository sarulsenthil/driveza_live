Frequently Asked Questions

What warranty do you provide?

All our vehicles come with a comprehensive warranty. We also offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind.

Can I inspect the vehicle before purchasing?

Absolutely! We encourage all buyers to thoroughly inspect any vehicle. We provide complete vehicle history reports and welcome third-party inspections.

Do you ship vehicles?

Yes, we can arrange shipping for vehicles throughout California and beyond. Contact us for a shipping quote to your location.

How do I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive by calling us, visiting our showroom, or using the contact form above. We'll arrange a convenient time for you to test drive any vehicle.
